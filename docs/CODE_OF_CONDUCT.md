# 🚀 How Your Churn System Works (Easy)

### 🧩 Step 1: Upload Data

* User uploads customer data (CSV file)
* Example: name, charges, tenure, etc.

---

### 🤖 Step 2: AI Predicts Risk

* Model checks each customer
* Gives **churn probability (0–100%)**

👉 Example:

* 85% → High chance to leave
* 20% → Low chance

---

### 📊 Step 3: Categorize Risk

System groups customers:

* **Low Risk** → <30%
* **Medium Risk** → 30–70%
* **High Risk** → >70%

---

### 🧠 Step 4: Find Reason

System explains **why customer may leave**

👉 Example:

* High charges
* Low usage
* Poor support

---

### 💡 Step 5: Suggest Action

System gives **what to do**

👉 Example:

* High charges → Give discount
* Poor support → Call customer

---

### 📈 Step 6: Show Dashboard

* Shows:

  * High-risk customers
  * Charts
  * Recommendations

---

# 🎯 Simple Flow

```text
Upload Data → AI Predicts → Risk Level → Reason → Action → Dashboard
```
